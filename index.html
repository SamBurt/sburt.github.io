About me

Education

In May 2018, I graduated from Colgate University magna cum laude with a degree in Computer Science. As well as graduating with honors in Computer Science, I also minored in History. My education taught me the value of examining problems from a variety of academic angles. I believe in the necessity of continuous intellectual pursuits to further my passions and career.

Technology

I am an extremely driven software engineer who believes in the power of the process of design to create products that validate the user's needs. I am a proficient programmer in Java, Ruby, Python, C, HTML and CSS. I have also done some work with javascript, golang and SQL. As a programmer, I believe in writing robust and concise code that works to solve novel problems. While at school, I worked on several projects that focussed on engaging with the community and using technology to solve a problem that was afflicting the community. I created applications using Ruby on Rails and the ionic platform that effectively and efficiently aided the community. I am passionate about technology and I am driven to learn and experience more.

Outdoors

Beyond me education pursuits, I work to spend as much time as I can in the outdoors and learning new skills. While at Colgate, I was part of the college's Outdoor Education program. I am a frequent hiker, canoeing and skier but I am always looking to try new things and expand upon my skill set.

Leadership
